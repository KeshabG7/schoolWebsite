Enrollment

Complete documentation and fee payment for admission

Eligibility Criteria

  • For Grade 6-8: Minimum 60% in previous class
  • For Grade 9-10: Strong aptitude in Science & Math
  • For Grade 11-12: Based on Grade 10 board results
  • Age criteria as per CBSE norms

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate (original + photocopy)
  • Transfer Certificate from previous school
  • Report cards of last 2 years
  • Passport size photographs (4 copies)
  • Aadhar card copy (student & parents)
  • Medical fitness certificate
