About Us

Demo School is dedicated to fostering research-oriented education and scientific excellence. We are committed to preparing students for careers in premier R&D organizations.

Our curriculum focuses on ISRO, DRDO, and advanced scientific fields including astrophysics, quantum physics, and cutting-edge research methodologies.

Our Mission: To cultivate scientific inquiry, innovation, and critical thinking in young minds, bridging the gap between academia and real-world research.

Principal's Message

Dr. Demo Deb

Dear Students, Parents, and Well-wishers,

It is my privilege to welcome you to Demo School, an institution committed to nurturing the next generation of scientists, researchers, and innovators. Our school embodies the spirit of scientific inquiry and excellence.

At Demo School, we believe that education is not just about acquiring knowledge, but about developing a research mindset, critical thinking abilities, and the courage to explore the unknown. Our curriculum is designed to prepare students for careers in premier research organizations like ISRO, DRDO, and academic institutions worldwide.

We provide state-of-the-art laboratories, expert faculty with research backgrounds, and opportunities for students to engage in real scientific projects. Our students don't just learn science--they live it, breathe it, and contribute to it.

I invite you to join us in this journey of scientific discovery and innovation. Together, we will shape the future of research and technology.

Warm Regards,
Dr. Demo Deb
Principal, Demo School
